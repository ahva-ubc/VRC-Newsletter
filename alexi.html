Alexi Paglinawan

Alexi Paglinawan is a second year MA student of Art History focusing on Philippine colonial art under imperial Spain. She has been working in the VRC since October of 2020 and, although you might not have seen her around due to the pandemic, she has been an important part of the team for her help in cataloguing and weeding out photograph slides in great numbers. With the academic year in full swing, she hopes to see more people come into the VRC to explore and utilize the space, as well as take advantage of the VRC's services
